About Project Sogand

This project is dedicated to the memory of Sogand Pakdel, a 27-year-old trans woman murdered in Kavar, Fars province, June 2025. Each point on this map documents a case of transfemicide or violence against trans and queer individuals in Iran. We honor their memory and work to shed light on systemic violence.

This map is an ongoing effort, continuously updated as new cases are verified according to strict privacy ethics.

Data & Methodology

Our dataset is compiled from verified news reports, human rights organizations, court records, and community testimony. Each documented case requires confirmation from at least two independent sources. Coordinates are rounded and names included only with official consent for privacy.
